What is Doubble?

Doubble by Sterling helps assure sustainability of your future cash flow in the medium term as there is no absolute guarantee of assured future cash inflows in the years ahead from existing sources. It allows you to save up now and earn returns as much as 100% of the amount saved which is paid monthly in equal installments to you, your spouse, kids or any beneficiary of your choice over a period of time after the savings period has ended. 

Watch your money grow and secure that future cashflow. With the options available, all you have to do is make your choice, relax and let us do the magic.

Variants

Doubble 3

This is a 72 months contract with 36 months contribution period (whilst savings accrue interest) and pay out with returns from month 37 to 60.

Doubble 5

This is a 120 months contract with 60 months contribution period (whilst savings accrue interest) and pay out with returns from month 61 to 120.

Doubble 10

This is a 180 months contract with 60 months contribution period (whilst savings accrue interest) and pay out with returns from month 61 to 180.

Doubble Lump Sum

This is a 120 months contract with an initial lumpsum payment (if you don’t earn monthly or you prefer to contribute a lump sum once) whilst accruing interest and monthly pay-out with returns starting from month 61 to 120.

Our Frequently Ask Questions

Can I pre-terminate my Investment?

Yes. The investment can be pre -terminated. However customers who pre-terminate before the end of the contract tenor will get their full contributions but will forfeit 50% of the accrued earnings.

Do I continue making contributions throughout the contract tenor?

No. You will only contribute for some months during the contract based on the selected variant after which the contribution stops and the pay-out to the nominated beneficiary with returns commences for remaining months in the contract.

How do I know what monthly pay-out I will get for different monthly contributions?

The calculator on the platform will state what the pay-out will be for the different contracts based on contribution. There will also be a contract letter stating the terms of the contact.

How do I know my balance?

You can view you balance on the platform using your log- in details.

Can I purchase multiples of the product?

Yes, you can open as many as you want for your benefit or other beneficiaries.

What is the target market for this product?

The product will benefit salary account holders, business owners, professionals and self - employed individuals between looking to save additional funds towards their later years for specific purposes.

Can I opt to collect the total contribution in lump sum instead of having the pay-out monthly over a period of time?

The contract is designed to pay out monthly over - time and not in bulk at the end of the contribution period as it is designed to address future cashflow sustainability challenge. Request to collect the contribution in lump sum will result in termination of the contract.

Is the beneficiary transferable?

Yes, to the new beneficiary the customer selects/ nominates for the pay-out period.

Can I just transfer any loose cash into my Doubble Contract?

No, the monthly indicated contribution is automated and is debited on the anniversary date every month.
